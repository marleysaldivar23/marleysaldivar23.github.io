A Modern Day Betamax Collection

After discovering Betamax tapes in the summer of 2024, Marley (a self declared film enthusiast) has become an avid collector and advocate for this forgotten home video format.

The first tape: Back to the Future (1985)

Pictured above: Replica of Marley's Back to the Future tape

Background: The first Betamax tape Marley purchased was Back to the Future! She decided to start her collection with Back to the Future because of its significance as a 1980s period piece (due to its depiction of 1980s fashion, politics and language), and the fact that it is her favorite film!

The collection consists of over 30 Betamax tapes, please be patient as we slowly add each tape to the site...
